SUGARLOAF MOUNTAIN

Located in sunny San Mateo, Sugarloaf Mountain is the ideal choice for families and hikers alike. Offering a variety of trails ranging in difficulty, visitors climb in elevation to a single trail that spans two cities, all with a full view of the Bay.

AUBURN CONFLUENCE

Starting off easy but leading you to nearly sixty degree slopes of dirt, the Auburn Confluence is suitable for both beginners and the more seasoned among us. Effort aside, the views on this Northern Californian trail will leave you conflu-stered.

SKYLINE to the SEA

Located in Big Basin, this daunting hike will bring you to the edge of your sanity. Descending from high in the mountains down to the sea, the trail showcases a variety of terrains and landscapes including forest, deserts, and waterfalls.

PLAN YOUR TRIP

HALF DOME

An insane hike where one false move could be death, Half Dome is a great experience for those wishing to conquer it. It requires a permit to hike, but it is worth it to pull yourself to the top of the rocky behemoth, sweating like an ewe in labor.
